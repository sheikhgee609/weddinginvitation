A Love Story

محبت کی داستان

Every great love story has a beginning, a journey, and a beautiful forever. Here is ours.

🌹
👀
Spring 2008
First Glance

At a mutual friend's gathering on Online, our eyes met across a crowded room. Neither of us could look away for long. A moment that changed everything.

📱
Summer 2009
Long Conversations

Hours turned into days of late-night calls and messages. We discovered we were more alike than either of us imagined — same dreams, same faith, same heart.

💍
December 2024
The Proposal

Under a garden full of fairy lights, Faiz went down on one knee. Suhada said yes before he even finished the sentence. The whole family was in tears of joy.

🌸
October 2025
Engagement Ceremony

Surrounded by family and the warmth of desi tradition, we made our promises official. The mehndi, the laughter, the tears — a day we will cherish forever.

🕌
December 2025
Forever Begins

Now we begin the most beautiful chapter — as husband and wife, under the blessings of Allah and the love of all who matter to us. This is just the start.
